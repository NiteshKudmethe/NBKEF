About Us

NBK Learners Edutech Foundation is a registered, MSME-certified educational platform committed to making quality technical education accessible and filled with opportunities for everyone, regardless of background or circumstances. We believe that empowering individuals with practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge is the key to unlocking their potential and driving social and economic progress. At NBK Learners, we provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment where students can gain hands-on experience in modern technologies, build in-demand skills, and connect with mentors and industry experts. Our mission is to bridge the gap between education and employment by delivering affordable, high-quality courses, workshops, and training programs designed to prepare learners for real-world challenges. We stand by our core values of integrity, innovation, and inclusivity — ensuring that every learner receives personalized guidance and opportunities to excel. Whether you’re looking to start a new career, upskill in your current role, or explore cutting-edge technologies, NBK Learners Edutech Foundation is your trusted partner on the journey to success.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join your programs and courses?

Our programs are open to students, freshers, job seekers, and working professionals who want to upskill in tech, freelancing, or entrepreneurship. No prior experience required for beginner tracks.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes! We offer certificates of participation, completion, and achievement. These are recognized by industry professionals and can strengthen your resume and LinkedIn profile.

Is there placement or career support after training?

Absolutely. We provide resume building, mock interviews, 1:1 mentorship, and active placement assistance through our partner network. Many of our learners land jobs and freelance gigs within weeks.

What kind of real-world projects do I get to work on?

We include hands-on, team-based project development across domains like web apps, backend systems, portfolios, APIs, and even startup MVPs. Projects are reviewed and can be added to your GitHub & portfolio.

How are sessions conducted and is it flexible?

We offer live online sessions, recorded modules, and flexible scheduling for personalized mentorship. All course content is accessible anytime to learn at your own pace.

Can I get guidance for research papers or open source?

Yes, we offer dedicated tracks for research writing support, publication roadmap, and guidance to contribute to open source, including Git, GitHub, and collaborative workflows.
